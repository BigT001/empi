## ğŸ’¡ Key Policy Points

### The "50% Rule"
- Customers pay 50% of rental as caution fee upfront
- This protects EMPI from damage/theft
- Customers get it back if items returned on-time & undamaged
- Fair to both parties

### The "5pm Deadline"
- All returns must be BEFORE 5pm on return date
- Even 1 minute late = 1 full day late penalty
- Allows time for inspection and 6pm refund processing
- Clear, firm deadline prevents disputes

### The "6pm Refund"
- All refunds processed at 6pm (end of business day)
- After inspection is complete
- Gives time to assess damage
- Same-day payment builds customer trust

### The "1/3 Rule"
- For each day late: lose 1/3 of caution fee
- After 3 days: entire caution fee forfeited
- Plus: daily rental charges for extra days
- Strongly discourages late returns

### The "Lagos Only" Rule
- Rentals limited to Lagos State currently
- Protects inventory and reduces risk
- Expansion planned to other states
- Clear to customers from start
